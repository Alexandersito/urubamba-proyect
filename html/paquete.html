Machu Picchu

Previous Next

From $650 / Min. 2 personas

We will pick up from your hotel in URUBAMABA or CUSCO at 05 :50 am and the day starts with a train ride from ollantaytambo to Aguas Calientes and from there we will accompany you to the bus that will take us up to the entrance of MACHUPICCHU about a 25 minute ride.

From there ,we will lead you on 4 hours in depth guided tour of the site and will teach you all about the history of how and why the inkas chose to build this magical place .if you have tickets to climb HUAYNA PICCHU or MACHU PICCHU MOUNTAIN ,your guide will accompany you on these climbs .if not ,we can hike to the sun gate or the inka bridge ,two lesser visited places at the citadel.

After the tour ,we will take you back to the bus station and accompany you back to your hotel in Aguas Calientes from there we will take a train to return to OLLANTAYTAMBO and finally by private car to Cusco
